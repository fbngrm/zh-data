## Header
grammar 1: 了 (le)
## Explanation
Verb + 了 indicates that this action has already happened.
## Syntax

## Note

## Examples

王老师来了。<br />
Wang2 lao3shi1 lai2 le5.<br />
Teacher Wang came.<br />

我吃了。<br />
wo3 chi4 le5.<br />
I ate.<br />

## Header
grammar 2: 了 (le)
## Explanation
Verb + 了 indicates that this action has already happened.
## Syntax

## Note
If 了 is followed by an object, a measure word, adjective or pronoun needs to come before the object.
## Examples

我同学买了一本书。<br />
wo3 tong2xue2 mai3 le5 yi4 ben3 shu1.<br />
My classmate bought a book.<br />

他吃了我的饭。<br />
ta1 chi1 le5 wo3 de5 fan4.<br />
He ate my food.<br />

## Header

## Note

## Examples

## Header

## Explanation

## Syntax

## Note

## Examples
