## Header
grammar 2: 什么 (shen2me5)
## Explanation

## Syntax
S + V + 什么 + N
## Note
In Chinese the sentence order of declaratives and questions are the same.
## Examples

你叫什么名字？<br />
ni3 jiao4 shen2me ming2zi?<br />
What's his name?<br />
