## Header
grammar 1: 会 (hui4)
## Explanation
会 refers to an ability that can be aquired by learning.
## Syntax
(不)会 + verb
## Note

## Examples

我会说汉语<br />
wo3 hui4 shuo1 han4yu3.<br />
I can speak Chinese.<br />

我不会说汉语<br />
wo3 bu2 hui4 shuo1 han4yu3.<br />
I can not speak Chinese.<br />

## Header
grammar 2: 哪 (na3)
## Explanation

## Syntax
哪 + measure word + noun
## Note
哪国人 means 哪个国家的人, the measure word 个has been omitted.
## Examples

哪个人<br />
na3 ge5 ren2<br />
which person?<br />

哪国人<br />
na3 guo2 ren2<br />
which country?<br />

哪个人是你的同学？<br />
Na3 ge ren2 shi4 ni3 de tong2xue2?<br />
Which one is your classmate?<br />

他是那国人？<br />
ta1 shi4 na3 guo2 ren2?<br />
Where does he come from?<br />
