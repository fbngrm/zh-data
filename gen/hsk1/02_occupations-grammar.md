## Header
grammar 1: 吗 (ma5)
## Explanation
吗can be placed at the end of a declarative sentence to indicate a yes/no question.
## Syntax
Declarative sentence + 吗？
## Note

## Examples

你是学生吗<br />
ni3 shi4 xue2sheng1 ma<br />
Are you a student?<br />

你喜欢王老师吗<br />
ni3 xi3huan Wang2 loa3shi1 ma<br />
Do you like teacher Wang?<br />

## Header
grammar 2: 吗 (ma5)
## Explanation
How to answer yes/no question?
## Syntax

## Note
1. You can simply use the verb in the sentence to answer! If the answer is affirmative, directly use the verb; if it's negative, use 不+verb.
2. Respond in a whole sentence.

## Examples

你是学生吗？是。<br />
Ni3 shi4 xue2sheng1 ma? shi4.<br />
;Are you a student? Yes.<br />

你是学生吗？不是。<br />
Ni3 shi4 xue2sheng1 ma?<br />
Ni3 shi4 xue2sheng1 ma? bu4 shi4;Are you a student? No.<br />

你是学生吗？我是学生。<br />
;Ni3 shi4 xue2sheng1 ma?<br />
Wo3 shi4 xue2sheng1;Are you a student? I am a student.<br />

你是学生吗？我不是学生。<br />
Ni3 shi4 xue2sheng1 ma? Wo3 bu4 shi4 xue2sheng1<br />
;;Are you a student? I am not a student.<br />
