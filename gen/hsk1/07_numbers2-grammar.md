## Header
grammar 1: 几 (ji)
## Explanation
ji is used to ask about a quantity below 10.
## Syntax

## Note
We normally use 几岁 to ask young children their age.
## Examples

他几岁? 他七岁。<br />
Tā ji suì? Tā qi suì<br />
How old is he? He is 7.<br />

## Header
grammar 2: 几 (ji)
## Explanation
ji is used to ask about a quantity below 10.
## Syntax

## Note
To ask for the date we normally use: ji yuè ji hào
## Examples

今天几月几号? 今天六月九号。<br />
Jīntiān ji yuè ji hào? Jīntiān liu yuè jiu hào.<br />
What's the date today? (Which month and day is it today?) Today is the 9th of June.<br />

## Header
grammar 1: 呢 (ne)
## Explanation
呢 is used as an omitted form in the interrogative sentence to avoid repeating the question.
## Syntax

## Note

## Examples

我是中国人,你呢?<br />
Wõ shì Zhīngguó rén, ni ne?<br />
I am Chinese. What about you?<br />

我六号去,王老师呢? 他八号去。<br />
Wõ liù hào qù, Wáng lăoshi ne? Tā bã hào qu<br />
I am going on the 6th, what about teacher Wang? He will go on the 8th.<br />
