## Header
在 (zài)
## Explanation

在 + place
## Note

## Examples

## Header
能 (néng)
## Explanation
能 indicates possibility or permission; It is often used in negative sentences or interrogative sentence.
## Syntax
在 + place
## Note
能 indicates possibility
## Examples

你六点能回家吗? 我六点能回家。<br />
Ni liù diăn néng huí jiā ma? Wõ liu diăn néng huí jiā<br />
Can you come home at 6? I can be come home at 6.<br />

妈妈,我能看电影吗?<br />
Māma, wŏ néng kàn diànying ma<br />
Mom, can I watch a movie?<br />

## Header
怎么 (zěnme)
## Explanation
is used to ask for reasons or methods. It is generally placed before the verb.
## Syntax
怎么 + verb
## Note
ask for methods; ask for reasons
## Examples

你怎么去学校? 我走路去学校 。<br />
Ni zěnme gù xuéxiào? Wŏ zõulù qù xuéxiào.<br />
How do you go to school? I walk to school.<br />

她怎么没去 学校? 她感冒了。<br />
Tā zěnme méi qù xuéxiào? Tā gănmào le.<br />
How (why) did she not go to school? She has a cold.<br />
