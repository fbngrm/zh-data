## Header
grammar 1: 有 (you3)
## Explanation
Indicates that in a certain place someone or something exists.
## Syntax
place + 有 + noun
## Note

## Examples

家里有一个西瓜。<br />
jia1 li5 you3 yi1 ge5 xi1gua1.<br />
There is one watermelon at home.<br />

## Header
grammar 1: 一点 (yi1dian3)
## Explanation
一点or 一点儿 indicates a small quantity or to a low degree; 一 is often omitted; 一点儿 (yi1dian3er) is nothern dialect.
## Syntax
place + 没有 + noun
## Note
can be a stand alone sentence.; can also be used before a noun to act as attribute.
## Examples

你们都吃一点。<br />
ni3men5 dou1 chi1 yi1dian3.<br />
We all ate a little.<br />

他喝了一点茶。<br />
ta1 he1 le5 yi1dian3 cha2.<br />
He drank a little tea.<br />
