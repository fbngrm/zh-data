## Header
grammar: 想 (xiăng)
## Explanation
想(xiăng) + verb indicates a wish or intention.
## Syntax
(不)想 + verb
## Note

## Examples

她想 喝咖啡。<br />
Tā xiăng hē kāfēi<br />
She wants to drink coffee.<br />

弟弟不想吃。<br />
Didi bù xiăng chí.<br />
The younger brother doesn't want to eat.<br />
