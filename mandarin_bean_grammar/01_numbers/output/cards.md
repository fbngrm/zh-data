<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>十 </strong>(shí) ten</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>十一 (shí yī) 11</p>
<p>二十 (èr shí) 20</p>
<p>三十一 (sān shí yī) 31</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>百</strong> (bǎi) hundred</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>一百 (yī bǎi) 100</p>
<p>一百<strong><u>零</u></strong>二 (yībǎi líng èr) 102</p>
<p>一百二 <strong><u>(十)</u></strong> (yībǎi èr shí) 120</p>
<p>一百二十三 (yī bǎi èrshísān) 123</p>
</blockquote>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>千</strong> (qiān) thousand</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>一千 (yī qiān) 1000</p>
<p>一千零二 (yīqiān líng èr) 1002</p>
<p>一千零二十 (yīqiān líng èr shí) 1020</p>
<p>一千零二十三 (yīqiān líng èr shí sān) 1023</p>
<p>一千三（百）(yī qiān sān bǎi) 1300</p>
<p>一千三百四（十）(yī qiān sānbǎi sì shí) 1340</p>
<p>一千三百四十五 (yī qiān sānbǎi sìshíwǔ) 1345</p>
</blockquote>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>万 </strong>(wàn) ten thousand</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>一万 (yī wàn) 10,000</p>
<p>一万<strong><u>零</u></strong>一 (yī wàn líng yī) 10,001</p>
<p>一万<strong><u>零</u></strong>二十 (yī wàn líng èrshí) 10,020</p>
<p>一万<strong><u>零</u></strong>二百 (yīwàn líng èrbǎi) 10,200</p>
<p>一万一<strong><u>（千）</u></strong> (yīwàn yīqiān) 11,000</p>
<p>一万一千三<strong><u>（百）</u></strong>(yīwàn yīqiān sānbǎi) 11,300</p>
<p>一万一千三百四<strong><u>（十）</u></strong>(yīwàn yīqiān sānbǎi sìshí) 11,340</p>
<p>一万一千三百四十五 (yīwàn yīqiān sānbǎi sìshíwǔ) 11,345</p>
<p>一万一千<strong><u>零</u></strong>二 (yīwàn yīqiān líng èr) 11,002</p>
<p>一万一千<strong><u>零</u></strong>二十三 (yīwàn yīqiān líng èr shí sān) 11,023</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>亿</strong> (yì) hundred million</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>一<span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0); --darkreader-inline-color:#ff9d41;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">亿</span>两千三百四十五万六千七百八十九 (yīyì liǎngqiān sānbǎi sìshíwǔ wàn liùqiān qībǎi bāshíjiǔ) 123,456,789</p>
<p>四<span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0); --darkreader-inline-color:#ff9d41;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">亿</span>五千万 (sì yì wǔqiān wàn) 450,000,000</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>Note</strong> that when there are several 0 together in between, you just need to say it out once.</p>
<p>In the multi-digit numbers, use 二，, not 两for tens place and ones place, as 22(二十二). Before the百(hundred)’s place, either 两 or 二 can be used, as in 200(二百/两百). When used before the “千”(qiān, thousand)、“万”(wàn, ten thousand) or “亿”(yì, hundred million) places, “两” is much more frequently used.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.&nbsp;</p>
<p>2000(两千);</p>
<p>22222(两万两千二百二十二);</p>
<p>200000000(两亿)</p>
</blockquote>
<h3>Ordinal Numbers</h3>
<p>The ordinal numbers indicate the sequence of a set of things, which show the rank or position. &nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><strong>第 + number</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>第一 (dì yī) the first</p>
<p>第三 (dì sān) third</p>
<p>第七 (dì qī) seventh</p>
</blockquote>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The ordinal numbers usually precede a measure word, but in certain cases, it could also precede a noun directly without the measure word and “第”.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>e.g.</p>
<p>二楼 (èr lóu) 2nd floor</p>
<p>三层 (sān céng) 3rd layer</p>
<p>13号楼 (13 hào lóu) Building 13</p>
<p>205房间 (205 fángjiān) Room 205</p>
<p>302路车 (302 lù chē) 302 Bus</p>
</blockquote>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Note</strong> that instead of “两”, “二”&nbsp;is used as an ordinal number, indicate the sequence.</p></div>
