Lesson 2
谢 文 的 家
Xiè Wén de Jia
Xie Wen's Family

A: 他是谁? 他是你朋友吗?
Tā shi shuí? Ta shi ni péngyou ma?
He is who? He is your friend?

B: 是,他是我朋友。他叫谢文,北京大学教书。你不认识他吗?
Shi,tā shi wo péngyou. Tā jiào Xiè Wén, zài Běijing . Dàxué jiāoshū. - Ni bú rènshi tā ma?
Yes, he is my friend. He (is) called Xie Wen, at Peking University teaches. You not know

A: 我听说过。他是北京人吧?
Wo tingshuöguo. rén ba? Ta shi Beijing
I heard say. He is Peking person? him?

B:不是,他是上海人。他爸爸是一个老工人。
Bú shì, ā shi Shànghǎi rén. Tả bàba shì yige lão göngren.
Not is, he is Shanghai person. His father is an old worker.

A:他家还有什么人?
Ti jiā jiā yếu hái you shénme rén?
His family still has what persons?
